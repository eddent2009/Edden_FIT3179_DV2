Fluoridation Status in Victoria

What is Water Fluoridation?

Water fluoridation is the controlled addition of fluoride to public drinking water to prevent tooth decay. Fluoride, a naturally occurring mineral, strengthens tooth enamel, making teeth more resistant to decay. Many communities add fluoride to their water supply as a public health initiative to reduce cavities, especially in children.

  • Fluoridated: Fluoride is added to the water supply to meet health recommendations.
  • Natural Fluoride: Fluoride is naturally present in the water without additions.
  • Partly Fluoridated: Some areas have fluoridated water while others do not.
  • Not Fluoridated: No fluoride is added to the water supply.
